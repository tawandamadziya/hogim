About Us

Our church, under the spiritual guidance of Pastor S Charamba, is a vibrant and devoted community of believers. We are united in our unwavering faith and commitment to serving God and our neighbors. In everything we do, we strive to reflect the love and teachings of Christ, welcoming everyone with open arms into our family of faith.

At the heart of our ministry is a deep dedication to worshiping God, growing in His Word, and living out our faith through acts of love and service. Pastor S Charamba leads us with a passion for the Gospel, inspiring us to seek God's presence in all areas of our lives. We encourage spiritual growth and foster strong, lasting connections among our members.

We invite you to join us for our weekly services, community events, and outreach programs. Together, under Pastor Charamba's leadership, we work to make a positive impact in the world, shining the light of God's love in every corner of our community.
